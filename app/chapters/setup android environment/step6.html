Configure the ADT plug-in

One more step, and you are done installing. Once you have installed the ADT plug-in, you will need to configure it. Installing the plug-in means that various parts of Eclipse now contain Android software development-specific dialogs, menu commands, and other tools, including the dialog you will now use to configure the ADT plug-in.

The step to configure the ADT plug-in is as followed:

  • In eclipe, select Window -> Preference (Linux and Windows) or Eclipse -> Preference (Mac)
  • Click the item labeled Android in the left panel of the Preference dialog.
  • Enter the path where you put the SDK
  • Click Apply
  • Your Android SDK installation is now completed.
