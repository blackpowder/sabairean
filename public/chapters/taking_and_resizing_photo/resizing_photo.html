Resizing Photo

In order to display the photo on your android application, you will need to resize your photo into the proper size and save in png extension. To do this, we would suggest the photo editor software which is called PhotoPad Photo Editor where you can download from this site http://www.nchsoftware.com/photoeditor/index.html

Here is step by step tutorial for resizing your photo.

  • Launch the PhotoPad Image Editor
  • Click on Open and Browse to the location where you store your photos
  • Once you have the photo, click on Crop to select the spot where you want to display
  • Click on Apply when you finish.
  • On the top bar menu click on Edit to start modifying your photo
  • Click on resize to change your photo size
  • In the scale box, type Width = 48 and Height = 48
  • After finish, Click on File -> Save As
  • In this window, browse to the location you want to store your photo and save it in png format
  • Click on Save to finish.
